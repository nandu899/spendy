Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: basic logs such as login timestamps and page visits.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically:

  • To display your expense history and summaries within the app.
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To diagnose errors and improve the reliability of the Service.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on servers in a secured environment. We take reasonable technical precautions — including access controls and encrypted connections — to protect your information from unauthorised access or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — to load typefaces used in the interface. Google may log font requests; see Google's privacy policy for details.
  • Hosting infrastructure — your data resides on our hosting provider's servers, which have their own data protection practices.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms, or social media trackers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the data we hold about you, please reach out via the contact information provided in the app. We will respond to all reasonable requests within a reasonable timeframe.
